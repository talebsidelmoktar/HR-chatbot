Components of a Stand-out Resume

Fantastic resumes come in a variety of styles and formats, but they all contain the same essential information. While the final appearance of your resume will vary depending on your requirements, these are the five essential components of a resume.

  • 01
Resume Header

Contains your contact information such as name Current and Permanent address (may be omitted from a resume posted on the web) Telephone number, E-mail address

  • 02
Professional Summary

Write your elevator pitch. This section should be 3-5 sentences long and contain information about the job title you seek, critical skills you possess, and professional achievements you’d like to highlight.

  • 03
Skills

List your hard and soft skills, customizing it to match the skills outlined in the job ad.

  • 04
Work history

List your jobs in reverse chronological order. This section will have more or less detail depending on the resume format you choose to use.

  • 05
Education

Outline your diplomas and degrees, as well as any academic honors you have been awarded.
